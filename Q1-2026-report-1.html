Top 6 Recommendations for Q2

  1. Improve AI Bot CSAT & Expand Scope
    Current CSAT 3.67 vs human 4.87 — focus training on product knowledge and common inquiries to both improve satisfaction and increase the share of tickets AI can handle. Target: 4.3+ CSAT, 20%+ of ticket volume. Frees CSR time for pre-sale conversion and phone support evaluation.
  2. Attack WISMO — Streamline Order Tracking
    ~960 tickets/quarter (40% of real volume). Build order status page, add realistic ETAs by product type, start tracking actual vs promised vendor delivery times. Streamlining the process also enables AI to eventually handle the bulk of WISMO inquiries. Frees CSR time for pre-sale conversion and phone support evaluation.
  3. Convert Pre-Sale Tickets into Revenue
    1,056 pre-sale tickets = people trying to buy. Track conversion rate separately. Route to trained agents. Better product pages (FAQs, install guides, stock status) reduce questions AND increase sales.
  4. Phone Channel Decision
    Van Land answers calls; CVHQ drops 100% (276/quarter). An estimated ~97 of those are pre-sale inquiries — potential buyers who never connect. Evaluate whether adding phone/callback support for CVHQ would pay for itself in captured sales.
  5. Vendor Accountability Scorecard
    6 vendors generate 60% of tagged support tickets. Cross-reference with Shopify revenue to calculate support cost per revenue dollar. Use in vendor negotiations and stocking decisions — hold vendors accountable for the support burden their products create.
  6. Close the Tagging Gap
    "Other" appears across reason, product, and vendor fields — these are tickets where more granular tags are needed. Better tagging = better data = smarter decisions. Review and expand tag taxonomy to reduce "Other" classification.
