Top 6 Recommendations for June

  1. Reduce WISMO at the Source
    ETA / status and lead-time questions still account for 178 tickets. Better product-page ETA language, proactive order updates, and clearer vendor lead-time ranges would directly reduce queue load.
  2. Improve AI Bot CSAT Before Expanding Scope
    AI Bot closed 276 tickets in May, but CSAT was 2.7 versus 4.8 for humans. Focus training on order-status, product Q&A, and handoff quality before asking it to absorb more volume.
  3. Turn Pre-Sale Traffic into Revenue
    273 tickets came from customers actively trying to buy. Better FAQs, comparison pages, install content, and lead-time visibility should reduce support load while helping conversion.
  4. Tighten Ticket Fields Completeness
    No Vendor, Other Vendor, No Product, and Product Misc still limit how actionable the monthly breakdowns can be. Cleaner Ticket Fields will make vendor and merchandising decisions more precise.
  5. Use the Named Vendor Burden List in Supplier Prioritization
    EcoFlow, CR Laurence, Van Windows Direct, Perfect Fit, and Fiamma account for a disproportionate share of named-vendor tickets and should stay near the top of supplier improvement conversations.
  6. Make a Clear Phone-Channel Decision
    A 39.4% dropped-call rate is too large to ignore. Either improve live coverage, offer a callback path, or steer more clearly toward digital channels that already resolve faster.
