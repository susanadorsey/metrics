Top 6 Recommendations for May

  1. Improve AI Bot CSAT & Expand Scope
    Current CSAT 3.0 vs human 4.6 — focus training on product knowledge and common inquiries to improve satisfaction and increase the share of tickets AI can handle.
  2. Attack WISMO — Streamline Order Tracking
    WISMO remains one of the largest support burdens in the report. Build an order status page, add realistic ETAs by product type, and track actual vs promised vendor delivery times.
  3. Convert Pre-Sale Tickets into Revenue
    Pre-sale tickets = customers actively trying to buy. Better product pages, FAQs, install guides, and stock visibility should reduce questions and support conversion.
  4. Close the Ticket Fields Gap
    “Other” remains material in vendor and product classification. Better Ticket Fields = better data = smarter decisions. We made progress on this in May, so this should improve in the next report.
  5. Vendor Accountability Scorecard
    A small number of vendors generate a disproportionate share of support tickets. Use that as a prioritization tool for supplier conversations and merchandising improvements.
  6. Phone Channel Decision
    Dropped calls remain material. Evaluate whether tighter deflection, callback support, or a clearer no-phone policy best matches the business goal.
