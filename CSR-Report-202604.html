Top 6 Recommendations for May

  1. Reduce WISMO at the source
    Make ETA, lead-time, and shipment-state visibility clearer on product and order-status surfaces so the largest preventable contact bucket shrinks.
  2. Improve AI on product and order questions
    AI volume is meaningful, but CSAT remains well below the human team. Train specifically on product questions, ETA explanations, and routing guardrails.
  3. Turn pre-sale demand into conversion support
    April had 308 pre-sale tickets. Better product comparison content, install guidance, and site UX can reduce support load while helping conversion.
  4. Tighten Ticket Fields for vendors and products
    Other Vendor and Product Misc still hide useful detail. Better Ticket Fields will improve downstream reporting and accountability.
  5. Use chat and self-serve for high-repeat questions
    Chat remains the fastest channel by a wide margin. Re-route repetitive inquiries there when possible and pair with clearer self-serve answers.
  6. Review high-burden vendors monthly
    EcoFlow, Perfect Fit, Van Windows Direct, and CR Laurence drove the most attributed vendor support volume in April, making them the best first candidates for root-cause review.
